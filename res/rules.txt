TikTakToe Rule Book

    1. A fair coin toss determines the player who is making the first move.
    2. The player who starts the game starts by placing an “X” in one of the unoccupied cells.
    3. The other player places an “O” in one of the unoccupied cells.
    4. The games ends when (a) the cells are occupied by “X”s or “O”s or when (b) a player has 3 of his symbols in a row. If neither player has 3 of his/her symbols in a row the game is a draw.
